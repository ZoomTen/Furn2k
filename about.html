Furnace is the ultimate chiptune tracker! With it you can create music using the finest quality emulation of more than 60 different sound chips from various gaming consoles. From the Atari 2600, NES, SNES, Genesis, and much, much more! Furnace has recently gained support for creating music using the OPL3 chip, so you too can make music that plays on your computer (through Adlib, Sound Blaster)!

Real-time Interface

Sporting a powerful yet extremely responsive interface, Furnace allows you to freely customize your workstation to your hearts' content, including the color scheme! With infinite setups, Furnace stays out of your way.

Export Options

Furnace also provides a variety of export options for use in games!

  • WAV export for general use
  • VGM export (covers >80% of the VGM specification!)
  • ZSM export for Commander X16

DefleMask™ Interoperability

Are you a DefleMask™ user? Use Furnace without leaving it entirely! Not only is the Furnace experience quite similar, it can also load and save all your songs in the DMF format, your DMF instruments, as well as your DMW wavetables!

Supports DefleMask™ files from Beta 3 to 1.1.9. Playback may differ slightly. DefleMask format saving available only for compatible chip configurations.

Furnace is NOT affiliated with Delek or DefleMask in any form, regardless of its ability to load and save the .dmf, .dmp and .dmw file formats. additionally, Furnace does not intend to replace DefleMask, nor any other program.
